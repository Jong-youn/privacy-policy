# Privacy Policy for 4-7-8 Breathing

_Last updated: [2026-07-31]_

This app ("4-7-8 Breathing", hereinafter "the App") is developed and operated by
**[YOUR NAME OR STUDIO]** ("we", "us"). We take your privacy seriously and want
to be transparent about the very small amount of data the App handles.

## 1. Information the App does NOT collect

The App has **no user accounts**, **no login**, and does not ask you for any
personally identifiable information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

- your name, email address, phone number, or postal address
- your location (GPS or coarse)
- your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
- your device advertising identifier (IDFA) for cross-app tracking

## 2. Information stored locally on your device

The App saves the following settings on your device using the operating
system's standard preferences storage (`UserDefaults` on iOS). This data
**never leaves your device** and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

- Preferred display language
- Number of breathing cycles per session

## 3. Information collected by third-party services

To keep the App working reliably we use two services provided by Google LLC
("Firebase"):

### 3.1 Firebase Crashlytics
When the App crashes, Firebase Crashlytics automatically sends a diagnostic
report so we can fix the problem. A typical report includes:

- the stack trace of the crash
- the version of the App and of your operating system
- your device model (e.g. iPhone 13)
- a randomly-generated, device-scoped installation ID that lets us count
  affected devices without knowing who you are

### 3.2 Firebase Analytics
Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage statistics that help us understand
which features are useful and which need improvement. Typical events include:

- App open / close
- Screen views inside the App
- Session length
- App version, OS version, device model, language, country (derived from IP,
  IP itself is not stored)

**IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) is not used.** The App does not track you
across other companies' apps and websites.

Google processes this data on our behalf and stores it on Google's
infrastructure. You can read more about Firebase's data handling here:
<https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>.

## 4. Legal basis and purpose

We process the data in Section 3 solely for the purposes of:

- diagnosing crashes and improving stability
- understanding aggregated usage to improve the App
- meeting our obligations to keep the service running

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with advertisers or data brokers.

## 5. Data retention

- Firebase Analytics data is retained according to Google's default retention
  policy (currently 2 months for user-level data, 14 months for event data).
- Firebase Crashlytics keeps crash reports for the period defined by Google's
  Firebase terms.
- We do not maintain any separate copies of the data outside of Firebase.

- **Opt out of analytics:** delete the App from your device. All Analytics
  events tied to that device installation stop immediately, and the
  installation ID becomes eligible for deletion under Google's policy.
- **Right of access / deletion:** you may email us at
  **[CONTACT_EMAIL]** to request access to or deletion of the data linked to
  your installation. Please include the approximate date and device model so

## 7. Children's privacy

data from children. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected such
data please contact us and we will remove it.
